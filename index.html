About Crumbs

Crumbs started in 2023 with a simple goal: to bring high-quality, homemade baked goods to our local community. What began as a weekend hobby quickly grew into a full-time passion fueled by love for food, creativity, and people.

We believe that baking is both an art and a science, and we take pride in using the freshest ingredients, traditional techniques, and a dash of innovation in everything we create.
